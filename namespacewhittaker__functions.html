Compute the Whittaker function.

The Whittaker function is defined in terms of the confluent hypergeometric function.

\[ W_{k,m}(z) = \mathrm{e}^{-z/2} z^{m+1/2} U\left(m-k+\frac{1}{2}, 1+2m, z\right) \]
