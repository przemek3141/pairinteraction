Compute radial matrix elements.

The radial matrix element can be calculated from the integral

\[ \langle nlj| \hat{p}^{\mathrm{rad}}_\kappa |n'l'j' \rangle = \int \Psi^{\text{rad}}_{nlj}(r) \Psi^{\text{rad}}_{n'l'j'}(r) r^{2+\kappa} \; dr \]

The part \( r^{2+\kappa} \) varies with rescaling the domain and is this given by the power_kernel function.
